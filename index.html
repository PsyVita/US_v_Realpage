Part II

The Plaintiff's Case

Sherman Act §§ 1 & 2

The Threat to American Renters

Landlords replaced competition with algorithmic price-fixing.

The Scheme

landlords → pool data → RealPage → rents on "autopilot"

Breaking the Law: The Data Moat Monopoly

  • §1 — Algorithm coordination = price fixing.
  • §2 — Data moat locks rivals out.
  • Remedy — renters overpay; dismantle the cartel.

Conduct, Harm & Conclusion

  • Data → RealPage → coordinated pricing.
  • Higher rents · vacancies.
  • Algorithms must not become a loophole.
  • Coordination via algorithm = Sherman Act violation.
